How It Works · Method 1
The 125% Rule.
Core Requirement
Post-closing assets ≥ mortgage debt × 1.25
Total mortgage debt = every mortgage you're personally liable for, including the new one
Post-closing = what's left after your down payment and closing costs are paid
All eligible assets count at 100% — including retirement accounts

= Loan amount $360,000
